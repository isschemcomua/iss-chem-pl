    Practical Steps
for sustainability

Small steps, real impact

Responsible Care, every day

Ethical sourcing

Customer choice

We embed sustainability into daily operations. We prioritize responsible local suppliers, offer a packaging take-back option, and encourage team ideas that save resources without compromising quality.
We adhere to REACH/CLP/GHS requirements, provide up-to-date SDS/TDS, label products, and train our personnel. We also ensure compliance and promote workplace safety.
We maintain a concise Supplier Code of Conduct and prioritize manufacturers with transparent safety and quality practices. Together with our partners, we reduce excess packaging and waste.
We offer alternatives with a smaller carbon footprint—different materials, packaging, and transport routes. Together with our customers, we choose solutions that preserve quality and conserve resources.
